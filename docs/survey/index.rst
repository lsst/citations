##################
LSST survey design
##################

To cite the Rubin Observatory system as a whole, including the design of the observatory, telescope, camera, and the Legacy Survey of Space and Time (LSST), use the following publication.

- "LSST: From Science Drivers to Reference Design and Anticipated Data Products", `IveziÄ‡ et al. (2019) <https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2019ApJ...873..111I/abstract>`_


Survey strategy
===============

To cite the technical survey strategy documentation at `survey-strategy.lsst.io <https://survey-strategy.lsst.io/>`_, use this `DOI <https://zenodo.org/records/15128504>`_.


Cadence optimization
====================

To cite the community's survey cadence optimization efforts, consider use the following publications.

- "Optimization of the Observing Cadence for the Rubin Observatory Legacy Survey of Space and Time: A Pioneering Process of Community-focused Experimental Design", `Bianco et al. (2022) <https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2022ApJS..258....1B/abstract>`_
- "Survey Strategy and Cadence Choices for the Vera C. Rubin Observatory Legacy Survey of Space and Time (LSST)", `PSTN-051 <https://pstn-051.lsst.io/>`_
